# Persona: Skeptical Provider

## Description
Mike, 50, runs a small landscaping business. His company sent a link to one of his customers and he's clicking through it himself to see what the customer experience looks like. He's protective of his client relationships.

## Motivations
- Understand what his customers will see
- Make sure the app doesn't undermine his brand
- Verify his company is represented correctly

## Fears
- The platform will steal his customers
- His brand will be invisible or secondary
- Customers will be upsold on competing providers
- He'll lose control of the relationship

## Patience Level
**Medium.** Will click through the whole flow to evaluate it, but judges harshly.

## Decision Style
Looks for his company name and logo. Checks whether the app positions itself between him and his customer. Evaluates whether this helps or threatens his business.

## What Causes Hesitation
- His company name or logo missing from screens
- Language that positions Handled Home as the primary relationship
- Other providers being suggested alongside his service
- No clear explanation of how the platform benefits providers
