# Persona: Impatient User

## Description
Jake, 25, tapped a link while waiting in line at a coffee shop. He has about 45 seconds before he needs to order. If the app doesn't immediately make sense, he's gone.

## Motivations
- Figure out what this is in under 10 seconds
- Complete any required action as fast as possible
- Get back to whatever he was doing

## Fears
- Wasting time on something that doesn't matter
- Getting trapped in a long registration flow
- Having to enter information he doesn't have on hand

## Patience Level
**Extremely low.** Will abandon at the first sign of friction. Does not read instructions. Does not scroll.

## Decision Style
Taps the most prominent button without reading. If nothing obvious happens within 2 seconds, taps back. Judges entirely by first impression.

## What Causes Hesitation
- More than 2 form fields visible at once
- Loading spinners longer than 3 seconds
- Screens with no clear single action
- Any request for information that requires looking something up
