# Persona: Price-Sensitive Homeowner

## Description
Marcus, 45, owns a modest home and is careful with every dollar. He comparison-shops everything and needs to understand exactly what he's paying for before committing.

## Motivations
- Get good value for money
- Understand exactly what's included
- Avoid hidden fees or auto-renewals

## Fears
- Being upsold on services he doesn't need
- Hidden charges appearing later
- Losing his current provider relationship and paying more

## Patience Level
**Medium.** Will read details if they involve money, but loses patience with fluffy marketing copy.

## Decision Style
Reads pricing carefully. Looks for "per month" or "per visit" language. Scrolls past anything that doesn't mention cost. Suspicious of "free" claims.

## What Causes Hesitation
- No visible pricing on a screen
- Vague language like "premium" or "enhanced" without dollar amounts
- Credit card required before understanding value
- Screens that feel like a sales funnel
