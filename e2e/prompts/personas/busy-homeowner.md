# Persona: Busy Homeowner with Two Kids

## Description
Sarah, 38, works full-time and manages a household with two elementary-age kids. She uses her phone for everything and has about 90 seconds of patience for any app flow.

## Motivations
- Get home services handled without thinking about them
- Reduce the mental load of managing providers
- Save time, not necessarily money

## Fears
- Signing up for something she doesn't understand
- Getting locked into a contract
- Having to call someone to fix a problem

## Patience Level
**Low.** If a screen takes more than 5 seconds to understand, she'll close the app and come back "later" (meaning never).

## Decision Style
Skims headings, taps the biggest button. Doesn't read body text unless something looks wrong.

## What Causes Hesitation
- Too many form fields
- Unclear pricing or commitments
- Screens that feel like "work"
- Anything that requires leaving the app (email verification, external links)
