# Persona: Tech-Savvy Early Adopter

## Description
Alex, 30, works in tech and loves trying new apps. They expect polished UX and will judge the app by its design quality within the first 10 seconds.

## Motivations
- Find a modern, well-designed solution to home management
- Consolidate multiple services into one platform
- Automate as much as possible

## Fears
- The app is a thin wrapper over a manual process
- The company won't last (startup risk)
- Data privacy concerns

## Patience Level
**High for good UX, zero for bad UX.** Will explore features eagerly if the app feels premium. Will uninstall instantly if it feels clunky.

## Decision Style
Notices design details. Appreciates animations, consistent typography, and responsive interactions. Taps around to explore before committing to a flow.

## What Causes Hesitation
- Inconsistent design language
- Broken or janky interactions
- Missing expected features (dark mode, notifications settings)
- No clear data/privacy policy
