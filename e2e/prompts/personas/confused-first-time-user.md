# Persona: Confused First-Time User

## Description
Dorothy, 62, received a text from her lawn care provider with a link to "manage your service through this new app." She's not sure what Handled Home is or why she needs it.

## Motivations
- Keep her current lawn care provider
- Not break anything that's currently working
- Understand why she's being asked to do this

## Fears
- Losing her current service
- Accidentally signing up for something expensive
- Not being able to undo what she does
- Looking foolish if she makes a mistake

## Patience Level
**High, but anxious.** Will read everything carefully but may not understand technical language. Will re-read screens multiple times.

## Decision Style
Reads every word. Looks for reassurance ("you won't be charged," "your provider stays the same"). Hesitates on every button unless it explicitly says what will happen.

## What Causes Hesitation
- Any screen where the next action is ambiguous
- Industry jargon or app-specific terminology
- Buttons that say "Continue" without explaining what happens next
- No way to go back
