**Handled Home — Module 13.3 PRD (Growth Autopilot: Market Launch OS)**  
**Mobile:** iOS + Android (Capacitor) — Customer + Provider (light surfaces)  
**Admin Console:** Operational control plane (primary)  
**Last updated:** 2026-02-22  

> **Big picture (context window)**  
> - **13.1** makes incentives safe (attribution/milestones/rewards).  
> - **13.2** makes providers self-serve and invite customers (“download the app” flywheel).  
> - **13.3** is the market brain: open/soft/waitlist/protect-quality by zone/category.  
> This module prevents viral growth from breaking quality.

---

## 0) Why this module exists

Virality is only good if quality survives.

Handled Home constraints:
- zones/capacity (Module 03)  
- service days/capacity (Module 06)  
- issues/credits (Modules 10–12 + Module 11)  

Module 13.3 introduces a health-based **Growth Autopilot** that:
- opens or throttles markets safely  
- adjusts incentives within hard caps  
- gives admins a clear control plane + “next best actions”

---

## 1) North Star outcomes

1) New markets can be launched with minimal human work.  
2) Growth opens/closes based on health (no overselling).  
3) Incentives adapt automatically within caps.  
4) Admin sees “what to do next” clearly.

---

## 2) Scope

### In scope
- market cohorts  
- zone/category readiness state machine  
- health scoring (deterministic first)  
- autopilot actions (gating + incentive dials)  
- admin growth console + overrides

### Out of scope
- ML forecasting  
- deep CRM tools  
- dynamic pricing automation

---

## 3) State machine per (zone, category)

States:
- `CLOSED`  
- `SOFT_LAUNCH`  
- `OPEN`  
- `PROTECT_QUALITY`

Transitions:
- CLOSED → SOFT_LAUNCH (minimum supply+demand met)  
- SOFT_LAUNCH → OPEN (stable quality across cycles)  
- OPEN → PROTECT_QUALITY (risk spikes)  
- PROTECT_QUALITY → SOFT_LAUNCH/OPEN (stabilized)

All transitions:
- explainable  
- logged  
- overridable by admin

---

## 4) Health scoring model (inputs + outputs)

Inputs:
- Supply readiness (providers ready, capacity)  
- Demand readiness (interest, conversion)  
- Quality risk (issues/credits/redo/overrides)

Outputs:
- sub-scores + overall `health_score`  
- label: stable / tight / risk

Thresholds must be admin-configurable.

---

## 5) Autopilot actions (what it can change)

Gating actions:
- open/close provider applications per category/zone  
- open/close customer waitlists  
- enable/disable Founding Partner slots  
- set launch-path targets (invite counts)

Incentive dials (within caps):
- adjust provider bonus tiers  
- adjust customer credits  
- adjust hold windows  
- adjust milestone strictness (first_visit vs paid_cycle)

Safety:
- cannot exceed caps  
- cannot override capacity realities  
- logs every action

---

## 6) Admin Growth Console

Recommended route: `/admin/growth`

Views:
- cohorts  
- zone/category matrix with states and scores  
- referral velocity  
- capacity pressure  
- quality risk

Includes “next best actions”:
- “Need 1 anchor provider in Zone X”  
- “Pause invites due to issue spike”  
- “Increase bonus tier within cap”  

Overrides:
- force state  
- lock state for N days  
- adjust thresholds/caps  
All audited.

---

## 7) Light in-app messaging

Customer:
- “Launching soon” / “Limited early spots”  
- “Invite your pro” callout when closed

Provider:
- “Open now” / “Waitlist + launch path” / “Soft launch”

No complex dashboards; keep it calm.

## 10) Acceptance tests

1) Health scores computed and stored on schedule.  
2) CLOSED → SOFT_LAUNCH transition logged correctly.  
3) SOFT_LAUNCH → OPEN after stable quality window.  
4) OPEN → PROTECT_QUALITY when risk spikes; incentives throttle.  
5) Admin override locks state; autopilot respects lock.  
6) No action exceeds hard caps; everything auditable.

---

## 11) Definition of done

Module 13.3 is done when the system can scale virally **without humans throttling growth**, while automatically protecting quality and capacity.
