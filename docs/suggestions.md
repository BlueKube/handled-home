# Suggestions

Product ideas, UX improvements, and business opportunities surfaced during development. This file is a funnel — not a backlog. Review periodically, promote good ideas to PRDs in `docs/upcoming/`, dismiss the rest.

## How suggestions get added

1. **During batch work (passive)** — The agent notices a gap, friction point, or opportunity while implementing. Appends a short entry before continuing. This is the most valuable source because the agent has deep context at that moment.
2. **End-of-phase review (semi-structured)** — After doc sync, the agent scans: "Given what I just built and what the north star docs say, what's missing?" Produces 2-5 targeted suggestions.
3. **On-demand audit (structured)** — Human asks for it, or a scheduled task runs it. The agent reads all 6 north star docs, scans the codebase, and produces a scored list organized by business impact.

## Entry format

```markdown
### [YYYY-MM-DD] Short title
**Source:** Where/when the idea came from (batch N, phase review, audit, etc.)
**Impact:** What business or user outcome this improves (retention, revenue, satisfaction, etc.)
**Effort:** Small / Medium / Large
**Tags:** relevant categories (e.g., retention, billing, customer-experience, provider-experience, growth-driver)
```
