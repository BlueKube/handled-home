---

## 0) Why this module exists

Your fastest path to density is **providers’ existing customer lists**.

Most independents are route-based, time-capped, and overwhelmed by texts.  
They light up for:
- fewer headaches  
- predictable money  
- looking more professional  
- getting paid for customers they already have  

Module 13.2 turns Founding Partner into a self-serve flow:
- provider downloads app  
- sees if their category/area is open  
- if open: onboard + start earning  
- if not open: “launch path” that rewards them for bringing customers  
- invite customers in seconds with a link + scripts  
- track progress and rewards without human involvement

---

## 1) North Star outcomes

1) Provider understands the offer in 10 seconds.  
2) Provider starts inviting customers in under 60 seconds.  
3) Provider can apply without calls or waiting (unless policy requires review).  
4) Customers receiving invites understand “download this app” + welcome credit + proof.  
5) The system routes providers into:
   - Live now  
   - Waitlist  
   - Launch Path (unlock)  
6) Providers feel safe: no “customer theft” vibe; rewards are milestone-based.

---

## 2) Scope

### In scope
- Provider Growth Hub (referrals + Founding Partner status)  
- Provider “apply” intake (lightweight)  
- Eligibility messaging by zone/category state  
- Invite customers tools:
  - magic link  
  - copy/paste scripts  
  - QR share (optional)  
- Invite other pros (optional MVP)  
- Progress tracking (invites → activations → first visits → rewards)  
- Minimal admin surfaces for partner assignment

### Out of scope
- Market health scoring logic (13.3)  
- Deep CRM pipeline tools  
- Contract workflows

---

## 3) Provider self-serve funnel (states)

### 3.1 Provider entry routes
- `/provider/referrals` (growth hub)  
- `/provider/apply` (application)

### 3.2 Provider application states
- `DRAFT`  
- `SUBMITTED`  
- `APPROVED`  
- `WAITLISTED`  
- `REJECTED` (rare; calm reason)

Prefer WAITLISTED + launch path vs hard rejection.

### 3.3 Category/zone readiness messaging
Provider selects:
- category  
- service area (zip list)

App returns:
- **Open now**  
- **Soft launch** (limited spots)  
- **Waitlist**  
- **Not supported**

Non-open states still show a next step:
- “Join launch list”  
- “Invite customers to accelerate launch”  

---

## 4) Founding Partner offer (in-product)

### 4.1 What they get
- Founding Partner badge  
- priority activation window (soft exclusivity)  
- higher bonuses for a time window  
- VIP support lane (optional)

### 4.2 How they earn (milestones)
Must use Module 13.1:
- customer subscribes  
- first completed visit  
- paid cycle (optional)

Copy must be explicit:
- “Paid after activation milestones, not invites.”

### 4.3 Launch path (when not live)
Examples (program-controlled):
- “Invite 10 customers to unlock priority activation.”  
- “Invite 25 to unlock Founding Partner tier.”

---

## 5) Invite customers (core loop)

### 5.1 Default tool: magic link + scripts
Route: `/provider/referrals/invite-customers`

UI:
- Primary: “Copy invite link”  
- SMS scripts (tap-to-copy):
  - short / friendly / professional  
- Optional: QR code display

### 5.2 Copy guardrails
No scheduling promises. Variables only.

### 5.3 Progress feedback
Provider sees:
- invites sent  
- installs  
- subs  
- first visits  
- bonuses pending/paid  
Plus “next best action.”

---

## 6) Customer invite landing flow

Customer taps link:
- “Your pro is moving updates to Handled Home.”  
- “Welcome credit when you activate.”  
- “Proof after each visit.”  
CTA: continue/signup.

After activation:
- optional: “Invite your other pro” (cross-pollination loop)

---

## 7) Provider → Provider recruiting (optional)
Simple:
- share link  
- reward after referred provider completes first job and brings N customers (program-controlled)

---

## 8) Admin controls (minimal)

- assign provider orgs to cohorts/programs  
- view partner funnel metrics  
- adjust templates and amounts (within caps)

---

## 9) Acceptance tests

1) Provider sees correct state (open/soft/waitlist) after entering zip/category.  
2) Provider can copy link and script in under 60 seconds.  
3) Customer signup attributed correctly (13.1).  
4) Provider sees milestone-based progress updates.  
5) Bonuses appear in provider payouts ledger (Module 11).  
6) Waitlisted provider sees launch path with progress tiers.

---

## 10) Definition of done

Module 13.2 is done when providers can self-serve the entire growth loop:
- apply  
- invite customers  
- track conversions  
- get paid on milestones  
…without humans bottlenecking growth.
