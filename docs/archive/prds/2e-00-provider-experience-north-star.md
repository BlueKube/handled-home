# PRD — Round 2E Provider Experience North Star

**Purpose:** Define the provider-side product thesis so every 2E wave ships toward the same outcome: **providers earn more with less stress** and it becomes **economically irrational to leave**—while the platform preserves the right to protect customer quality.

**Scope:** This is the short “anchor PRD” for all 2E waves. Each execution PRD (Founding Partner, Day Command Center, Earnings Trust, Availability/Coverage) should reference and conform to the principles below.

---

## 1) Provider Promise (what we sell)

Providers should feel better off joining Handled Home because they:

1. **Get demand automatically** — jobs are fed to them (no door knocking, no ads, minimal selling).
2. **Have an optimized day** — every morning the app provides a complete plan: route, job order, time windows, checklists, and proof requirements.
3. **Earn through merit** — quality + reliability unlock more volume and better routes; poor performance reduces work.
4. **Increase monthly income** — even if per-job pay is slightly lower, utilization is higher (less dead time/drive time).
5. **Avoid admin work** — payments, collections, customer comms, reminders, and earnings reporting are handled by the platform.

---

## 2) Platform Non-Negotiables (customer protection)

- Customers belong to the **platform**, not the provider.
- Providers can be **Primary** for a zone+category, but “Primary Rights” are **earned and revocable**.
- Customers can request provider changes.
- The platform can reassign due to: quality failures, availability failures, safety issues, fraud, or repeated policy breaches.
- Vacation coverage is normal: backups cover; quality must not drop.

---

## 3) Key Systems (how supply retention is actually achieved)

### 3.1 Day Command Center
The provider “Today” experience must be so good that providers build their business around it:
- route map + ordered stops
- one-tap navigation
- status flow + proof
- live earnings today + week projection
- exceptions explained in one sentence

### 3.2 Quality Score & Tiers
Providers are managed via a **stable, explainable Quality Score**:
- **Rolling 28-day** score window
- **Weekly** coaching rollups (provider-anonymous)
- Score affects assignment priority, overflow eligibility, hold periods, and tier status
- Score must be explainable and actionable

### 3.3 Earnings Trust
Providers stay when they trust pay:
- per-job breakdowns
- modifiers transparency
- holds + release countdown
- payout schedule clarity

### 3.4 Availability + Coverage
Providers need flexibility, but the platform needs reliability:
- vacation blocks respected by assignment
- automatic coverage via backups
- lead-time and policy guardrails to prevent quality collapse

### 3.5 Founding Partner (BYOC)
Providers can bring customers onto the platform because it’s better for both sides:
- customer gets card payments + receipts + proof + schedule predictability
- provider gets a **time-limited CAC bonus** + easier collections + reduced admin burden

---

## 4) What “winning” looks like (success metrics)

**Provider retention:** 90-day, 180-day  
**Utilization:** work minutes / available minutes  
**Drive efficiency:** drive minutes per stop  
**Quality:** issue rate, redo rate, photo compliance, on-time rate  
**Automation:** % days run without admin involvement  
**Supply growth:** providers invited, approved, and activated per week  
**BYOC:** invited customers → installed → subscribed → completed first service week

---

## 5) Deliverables (2E PRD set)

- **2E-01 Founding Partner Program (BYOC)** — acquisition incentive + attribution + payout + anti-fraud
- **2E-02 Provider Day Command Center** — Today + route + status flow + map
- **2E-03 Earnings Trust & Analytics** — earnings breakdown, holds, modifiers, projections
- **2E-04 Availability & Coverage** — vacation blocks, days off, reassignment rules

---

## 6) Out of scope for 2E (defer to Admin/Economics rounds)
- Full pricing engine control center
- Deep gamification (leaderboards/badges) beyond tier banding
- Public provider marketplace profiles
